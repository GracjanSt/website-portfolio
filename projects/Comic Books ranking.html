My favourites Comic Books - Top 4

  1. Batman: White Knight

    By Sean Murphy
    Sane Joker? What more could you want?

  2. Batman Noir: The Court of Owls

    By Greg Capullo and Scott Snyder
    Two creatures of the night in price of one? I'm in.

  3. Saga Of The Swamp Thing

    By Alan Moore
    When nature shows its predatory face, with a little bit of dark magic.

  4. Batman Metal - whole saga

    By Scott Snyder
    Batman and Joker as one person. There is nothing more to say.
